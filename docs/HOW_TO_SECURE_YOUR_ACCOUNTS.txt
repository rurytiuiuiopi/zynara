# HOW TO SECURE YOUR ACCOUNTS LIKE A PRO
# =========================================

## STEP 1 — Enable 2FA on GitHub
1. Go to: github.com
2. Click your profile picture → Settings
3. Click "Password and authentication"
4. Click "Enable two-factor authentication"
5. Choose "Authenticator app"
6. Download Google Authenticator on your phone
7. Scan the QR code
8. IMPORTANT: Download and print the recovery codes
9. Store them with your MASTER_ACCESS.txt document

## STEP 2 — Enable 2FA on Railway
1. Go to: railway.app
2. Click your profile picture → Account Settings
3. Find "Two Factor Authentication"
4. Enable it using Google Authenticator
5. Save recovery codes

## STEP 3 — Secure Your Email
Your email is the master key to everything.
If someone hacks your email, they can reset all passwords.
1. Go to your email settings
2. Enable 2FA/Two-Step Verification
3. Use a strong password (not the same as anything else)

## STEP 4 — Get a Password Manager (Free)
Download Bitwarden: https://bitwarden.com
1. Create one master password (make it very strong)
2. Store all your other passwords inside it
3. Never forget a password again
4. Works on iPhone, Android, computer

## STEP 5 — Make Your GitHub Repos Private
1. Go to: github.com/rurytiuiuiopi/shatta---fans
2. Click "Settings"
3. Scroll to bottom → "Danger Zone"
4. Click "Change visibility" → "Make private"
5. Repeat for: github.com/rurytiuiuiopi/zynara

## STEP 6 — Check Your Domain Ownership
1. Go to wherever you bought shattamovementfanbase.com
2. Make sure it is registered in YOUR name
3. Make sure YOUR email is the contact
4. Enable auto-renewal so it never expires
5. Write the expiry date in your MASTER_ACCESS.txt
