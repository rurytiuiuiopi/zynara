║         ZYNARA – Deploy to Railway (Permanent Link)         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Railway gives you a FREE permanent link like:
  https://zynara-production.up.railway.app

STEP 1 – Create a free GitHub account (if you don't have one)
  https://github.com/signup

STEP 2 – Upload your zynara folder to GitHub
  1. Go to https://github.com/new
  2. Name it "zynara", set to Private, click Create
  3. Click "uploading an existing file"
  4. Drag your entire zynara folder contents in
  5. Click "Commit changes"

STEP 3 – Deploy on Railway
  1. Go to https://railway.app
  2. Click "Start a New Project"
  3. Click "Deploy from GitHub repo"
  4. Sign in with your GitHub account
  5. Select your "zynara" repo
  6. Railway auto-detects everything and deploys it!

STEP 4 – Get your public link
  1. In Railway dashboard, click your project
  2. Click "Settings" → "Domains"
  3. Click "Generate Domain"
  4. Your link appears — share it with fans!

STEP 5 – Add your .env variables on Railway
  1. In Railway dashboard, click your project
  2. Click "Variables"
  3. Add each line from your .env file here
  (This keeps your MoMo keys safe and secret)
